About Me

Sound Engineer with big a passion for surfing, skateboarding and travelling.

I am a self-motivated audio engineer with a professional background in post production for film, TV and Games. Following on from my degree in sound engineering I have worked both freelance and within a facility as an audio professional. I have spent the past five years working in the editorial and mixing departments of several independent film productions and, during the past year I worked as Assistant Sound Editor in-house at Phenomen films UK.
